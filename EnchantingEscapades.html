ANDAMAN AND NICOBAR ISLANDS

Andaman and Nicobar Islands is a group of beautiful islands in India’s Bay of Bengal, South-East of the Indian mainland.
The islands offer a picturesque view and are covered with white sand. There are a number of pristine beaches, rich coral ecosystems, and has one of the best dive sites.
The islands shine like a gem in the blue waters of the Bay of Bengal.

Culture
Andaman and Nicobar Islands have a unique culture where all religions, languages, ethnic groups live in total peace and harmony and hence it is rightly called Mini India by some.
Port Blair is one of the major islands and being the capital of the Andamans has a cosmopolitan character where people from all religions and walks live together.
All festivals are celebrated with equal enthusiasm and zeal and attended by all tourists and the local population.
